Development Approach

With the rapid development of digital technology, online shopping is becoming a new trend that is inevitable. Now we can buy almost anything through our smartphones. Food is no exception. And they are also frequent users of online food ordering services, so it was also an idea for them to decide to create a fast food ordering and delivery app.

Two objects are targeted: the owner's shop and the person who wants to order food. Shop owners can register an account to post their dishes for sale on the app, and users can search for and order food conveniently.
